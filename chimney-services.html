Our Services

Chimney Services
Done Right

Chimney Cleaning

Annual cleaning removes creosote, soot, and debris — reducing fire risk and improving draft performance.

  • Full flue sweep
  • Firebox cleaning
  • Smoke chamber brushing
  • HEPA vacuuming — no mess

Chimney Inspection

Level 1, 2, and 3 NFPA 211-compliant inspections with written report. Camera inspections available.

  • Level 1 — visual inspection
  • Level 2 — camera inspection
  • Written condition report
  • Repair estimates included

Chimney Cap Installation

Prevent rain, animals, and debris from entering your chimney with a properly fitted stainless steel cap.

  • Stainless steel caps
  • Custom-fit to flue size
  • Draft-enhancing designs
  • Lifetime warranty options

Flashing Repair

Leaking around your chimney? Damaged or improperly installed flashing is the #1 cause. We reseal and replace.

  • Step flashing replacement
  • Counter flashing repair
  • Sealant application
  • Leak source diagnosis

Vent System Services

Gas appliance vents, B-vent systems, and dryer vent cleaning — all properly sized, routed, and installed.

  • B-vent & flex liner
  • Dryer vent cleaning
  • Vent cap installation
  • Code compliance checks

Damper Repair & Replace

Stuck, broken, or missing damper? We repair or replace throat and top-mount dampers for proper draft control.

  • Throat damper repair
  • Top-mount damper install
  • Poker & handle repair
  • Energy-seal dampers
