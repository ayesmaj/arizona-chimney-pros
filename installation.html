The Right Fireplace
For Every Home

Gas Fireplace Inserts

Convert your existing wood fireplace into an efficient, beautiful gas fireplace. No chimney reconstruction needed.

  • Fits existing firebox opening
  • Gas line connection included
  • Variety of log & flame styles
  • Remote or wall switch operation

Direct Vent Fireplaces

A sealed combustion system that vents through an exterior wall — perfect for interior rooms without existing chimneys.

  • No existing chimney required
  • Highly efficient combustion
  • Interior & exterior locations
  • Glass front for full flame view

Ventless Gas Fireplaces

No venting required. Install virtually anywhere in your home with a stunning flame and maximum heat output.

  • 100% of heat stays indoors
  • Install in any room
  • No duct or pipe routing
  • Realistic flame appearance
